# Kamgar Connect — Complete Product Information

## What is Kamgar Connect?

Kamgar Connect is a digital platform that connects daily wage workers (kamgars) with employers in tier-2 Indian cities, starting with Aurangabad (Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar), Maharashtra. The name "Kamgar" means "worker" in Marathi and Hindi.

It solves a decades-old problem: every morning, hundreds of daily wage workers — masons, electricians, plumbers, domestic helpers, factory workers — gather at street corners called "chowks" and wait for hours hoping someone will come and hire them. Employers also waste time driving to chowks hoping to find the right worker. Kamgar Connect eliminates this inefficiency by bringing both sides onto a single mobile-friendly platform.

---

## The Problem It Solves

### For Workers:
- Wasted hours standing at chowks every morning with no guarantee of work
- No way to showcase their skills or build a reputation
- Payments are often delayed, unfair, or opaque
- No job history or verified record of past work
- Completely dependent on word of mouth

### For Employers:
- No reliable way to find skilled workers quickly
- Have to physically go to chowks and negotiate on the spot
- No way to verify a worker's skills, reliability, or past work
- No transparent payment system
- Risk of hiring unskilled or unreliable workers

---

## The Solution

Kamgar Connect is like an Uber for daily wage labor. Workers create a verified digital profile with their skills, experience, location, and availability. Employers post job requirements. The platform uses GPS to match the nearest available verified worker to the employer instantly — all from a smartphone.

---

## Core Features

### For Workers:
- Free profile creation with skills, photo, location
- Instant job alerts on phone via SMS or app notification
- GPS-based job matching — get jobs near your location
- Transparent digital payments via Razorpay
- Ratings and reviews to build reputation over time
- Job history and verified work record

### For Employers:
- Post a job in under 2 minutes
- Browse verified worker profiles by skill and location
- GPS map showing nearest available workers
- See ratings, reviews, and past work history
- Pay securely via Razorpay — no cash hassles
- Rate workers after job completion

---

## How It Works — Step by Step

1. **Sign Up** — Register as Employer or Worker in 2 minutes, verify phone number
2. **Create Profile** — Workers add skills, photo, location; Employers add company/home details
3. **Post or Browse** — Employers post job requirements; Workers browse available jobs nearby
4. **GPS Matching** — Platform instantly shows nearest available verified workers
5. **Connect** — Employer contacts worker directly via call or in-app message
6. **Work** — Worker completes the job
7. **Pay** — Employer pays digitally via Razorpay, transparent and on time
8. **Rate** — Both parties rate each other, building a trust ecosystem

## Target Market

### Primary Market:
- Tier-2 cities in Maharashtra — Aurangabad, Nashik, Kolhapur, Solapur, Nagpur
- Later expanding to all tier-2 cities across India

### Workers (Supply Side):
- 18–55 year old male and female daily wage workers
- Low to medium tech literacy — platform must be extremely simple
- Primarily use basic Android smartphones
- Speak Marathi and Hindi

### Employers (Demand Side):
- Homeowners needing domestic help, repairs, or construction
- Small to medium construction contractors
- Factory and warehouse owners
- Local businesses needing daily wage staff
